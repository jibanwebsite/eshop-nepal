Shipping & Delivery Process

1. Order Processing

Once you place your order, our team verifies your payment and prepares your items for shipment. You will receive a confirmation email with your order details and expected delivery time.

2. Packaging

All orders are carefully packaged to ensure your products arrive safely. Fragile items are securely cushioned to prevent damage during transport.

3. Shipping

We offer Standard, Express, and Overnight shipping options. Delivery times vary based on the selected method and your location. You will receive a tracking number to follow your shipment in real-time.

4. Delivery

Your order will be delivered directly to your doorstep. For secure delivery, a signature may be required. In case of missed delivery, our courier partners will attempt redelivery or leave pickup instructions.

5. Returns & Support

If you are unsatisfied with your order, our returns process is simple and hassle-free. Contact our support team for guidance, and we will provide a prompt solution or replacement.
